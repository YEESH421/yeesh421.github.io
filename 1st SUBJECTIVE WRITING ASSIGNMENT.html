    1st SUBJECTIVE WRITING ASSIGNMENT (25 points)

    1.5 single spaced pages, with additional images to submit on Carmen drop box by August 29.

    Identify one or two images (preferably ads, but not necessarily) that portray an idea of beauty and respond subjectively: offer your thoughts, emotions, beliefs. Start with the image(s) and move on to talk about yourself. You may focus on body/face images as we did on the first day of class or on other subjects (cars, buildings, nature etc.) as long as you "feel" they express a perception of beauty that speaks to you. In your analysis remember to consider your 5 senses (sight, hearing, touch, smell, and taste)  when applicable. Furthermore, convey both your emotional and intellectual understanding of the images to provide a more comprehensive analysis.

    There is no rule to follow, just be as creative as you can. You are in charge! It's important, however, not to repeat or contradict yourself. Be as clear and concise as possible. Do not lose yourself in generalizations or hyperboles.

     

    What I expect:

    a clean and solid essay which means an essay that shows good style and content: perfect grammar and good depth of perception.The introduction should provide time and space coordinates (when and where was the image published?) and a focused and debatable thesis (a fact is not a thesis). Start with the description/analysis of the image and them move on to your opinions/reactions/emotions or any other form of personalized writing. Yes, you may speak in 1st person ("I") but avoid repeating "I feel," "I think," "in my opinion" etc. Throughout the essay use the present tense except when you discuss a past time period related to either you or the image.
