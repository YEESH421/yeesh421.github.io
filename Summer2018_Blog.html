Summer 2018

As summer comes to a close, it's time to reflect on how I spent my time.

May

May started out with me getting my first full time job ever. I worked at a pizza place on a tiny island that my girlfriend happens to live on. I lived in a dorm by myself, which was nice, but the internet was pretty bad pretty much anywhere on the island. On top of that, my girlfriend was in Eruope vacationing, so there wasn't much for me to do in my freetime - which I didn't have a surplus of anyways since I was spending 6 days a week at work. When I did find some spare time, I went fishing. I had actually forgotten my fishing pole at home, so my first day attempting to fish, I tried making a pole out of a stick, some string I found on the beach, and a broken soda can tab. Luckily, I ran into a charitable old man who was willing to trade my trash pole for a real one. I caught this fish with the pole he gave me, but as it turns out, the fish was a smallmouth bass and it was illegal to fish them, as it was spawning season. I promptly released the fish but I was nevertheless proud to have caught my first fish in many years.
